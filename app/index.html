Welcome at Chitta


Chitta is an effort to make land ownership public and disputable. Everyone is able to anonymously inspect land owership without any friction.

Our goal is to create an impermuable ledger that protects small land ownsers, and stimulates fair use of land. Do you have rights on land? Claim the unquie Chitta key token.

All you need to do is answer a few basic questions, receive peer review and optionally verify with an official land registry document.

Benefits of a being a tokenholder :

  • Free membership of a regional land assocation.
  • Discount on products you use on your tokenized land.
  • Better sale prices for what your tokenized land products.
  • Support on legal land issues.
