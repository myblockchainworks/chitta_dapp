Welcome at Arani land association. Together with all land owners in the Arani region, we support each other by sharing information, voting on rulings and find solutions for double registrated land. Each member has one vote only.

Sometimes we will ask you to vowe for new owners, or vote on an amendment to our association. We will notify you when and within what time frame we ask you to act.
